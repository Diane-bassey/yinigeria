About YIN

Young Innovators of Nigeria Social Organization (YIN) is a social impact organization with key emphases in IT innovations and research, mobile technology businesses, current trends in innovations with key focus on indigenous Technology to foster national development. YIN started operations in 2014 and all our programs and events are designed, implemented & carried out by Young Innovators across Nigeria. Read More
